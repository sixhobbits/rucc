USSA 2012

2 December to 8 December 2012, Port Elizabeth

USSA 2012 was hosted by NMMU. We unfortunately had a lot of last minute drop-outs from our team, due to family crises and health issues, but the small dedicated group of five players who played in the tournament had a great time, and kept Rhodes on the chess map. This was a five day tournament, and we played 9 games, with two games per day, and one on the Wednesday. On Wednesday evening there was also a blitz tournament, which was great fun.

The players who went up were:
Gareth Dwyer
Xolani Mpolongwana
Langelihle Ngwenya
Sebenzile Sopazi
Kudatinashe Makura

As a team, our final ranking was 18th out of 22 teams, with 19.5 points in total. This may see may seem a bit disappointing, but considering that most institutions had 15 players to our 5 makes it a little more impressive. Furthermore, some of the team had never played in an open tournament before, so this was a brilliant experience for them, and it was great to see how much their games improved between the beginning and the end of the event.
